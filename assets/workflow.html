Exhibition Management

Guiding you through each step to ensure a seamless exhibition experience.

Initial Consulting

6-8 Months Before
  • Contact our team to schedule an initial meeting.
  • Discuss exhibition goals and requirements.
  • Ensure we fully understand your vision.

Design and Strategy

4-6 Months Before
  • Review booth size and design concepts.
  • Confirm logistics, materials, and setup requirements.
  • Confirm attendee schedules and travel arrangements.

Pre-Event Coordination

1 Month Before
  • Finalize logistics and shipping details.
  • Discuss on-site requirements and setup team.
  • Conduct a final review of the booth setup process.

Event Coordination

During and After Event
  • Provide on-site setup and support throughout the event.
  • Assist with live marketing and networking efforts.
  • Facilitate live streaming from the booth if required.
  • Collect materials and compile post-event reports.

Market Entry Support

From initial research to establishing a local presence, our support ensures a successful entry into new markets.

Initial Consultation and Research

Six-Eight Months Before
  • Schedule a consultation to outline market entry goals.
  • Conduct market research to understand the landscape and opportunities.
  • Identify target audience and competitor strategies.

Strategy Development

4-6 Months Before
  • Develop a market entry strategy.
  • Plan for business matchmaking and partnership opportunities.
  • Conduct cultural and language guidance sessions.
  • Engage in Q&A to refine the strategy to your needs.

Local Setup and Networking

2-3 Months Before
  • Begin setting up local offices.
  • Assist with networking meetings and event scheduling.
  • Build relationships with local partners.

Final Preparations

1 Month Before
  • Conduct a final review of the market entry plan.
  • Provide cultural and language training for your team.
  • Confirm all local logistics.

Event Management

Ensuring that your events, from networking receptions to leisure activities, are seamlessly organized.

Concept Planning

4-6 Months Before Event
  • Discuss event goals and desired outcomes.
  • Brainstorm ideas for activities, workshops, or networking events.
  • Finalize the event concept and timeline.

Schedule refining

2-3 Months
  • Secure venues and finalize event schedules.
  • Confirm guest lists and speaker invitations.
  • Engage in Q&A sessions to ensure alignment with goals.

Pre-Event Coordination

1Month Before
  • Review logistics and finalize all details.
  • Conduct a pre-event coordination call with your team.
  • Ensure all materials and staff are prepared .

Event Execution

During and After Event
  • On-site coordination and support throughout the event.
  • Facilitate networking and guest engagement activities.
  • Collect attendee feedback and compile post-event reports.
