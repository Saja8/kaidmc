Workflow

Consulting

6-8 Months Before
  • Initial meeting to understand your business needs.
  • Define the project scope based on your objectives.
  • Develop a customized approach for your exhibition, market entry, or event.

Planning

4-6 Months Before
  • Design the concept, plan logistics, and finalize strategy.
  • Coordinate travel, booth design, and local setup.
  • Ensure smooth integration of market entry or event features.

Execution

1 Month Before and Onsite
  • Execute the plan with on-the-ground support for events or exhibitions.
  • Oversee logistics, attendee management, and exhibitor or partner engagement.
  • Provide post-event support, including feedback and reporting.

Follow-up

Post Event
  • Evaluate the event or market entry impact.
  • Provide detailed reports on ROI, engagement, and market insights.
  • Offer strategic recommendations for future events or expansions.
