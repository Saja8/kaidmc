Welcome to KaiLinks

Founded with a vision to connect businesses across borders, KaiLinks takes inspiration from the kanji "Kai" (界), meaning "world". This reflects our commitment to bridging global markets and creating opportunities for businesses to thrive internationally.

Our mission is to empower businesses to expand their reach by providing seamless and effective exhibition and market entry solutions. We strive to build bridges across borders, making global connections accessible for companies of all sizes.

About Us

Our team of professionals brings together diverse expertise in exhibition management, logistics, and international business.

With roots in Mexico and Japan, we support organizations working between Japan and Latin America from early planning to on-site execution and follow-up.

Workflow

Consulting

6-8 Months Before
  • Initial meeting to understand your business needs.
  • Define the project scope based on your objectives.
  • Develop a customized approach for your exhibition, market entry, or event.

Planning

4-6 Months Before
  • Design the concept, plan logistics, and finalize strategy.
  • Coordinate travel, booth design, and local setup.
  • Ensure smooth integration of market entry or event features.

Execution

1 Month Before and Onsite
  • Execute the plan with on-the-ground support for events or exhibitions.
  • Oversee logistics, attendee management, and exhibitor or partner engagement.
  • Provide post-event support, including feedback and reporting.

Follow-up

Post Event
  • Evaluate the event or market entry impact.
  • Provide detailed reports on ROI, engagement, and market insights.
  • Offer strategic recommendations for future events or expansions.
