KaiLinks coordinates host institutions, daily schedules, transport, and on-site support for universities and schools running academic modules or institutional delegations outside their home base.

## Program Scope

- **Academic matchmaking**: campus visits, guest classes, and faculty introductions.
- **Learning itineraries**: balanced schedules that combine academic outcomes, technical visits, and cultural activities.
- **STEM and industry visits**: technical site access based on your field priorities.
- **Student logistics**: transport, accommodation, meal planning, and safety support.
- **On-ground coordination**: bilingual team support during every activity block.

## Common Program Formats

- Credit-bearing international modules.
- Faculty-led short courses.
- Institutional delegations and partnership scouting visits.
