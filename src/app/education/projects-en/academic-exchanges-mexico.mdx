For institutions running a program in Mexico for the first time, KaiLinks handles partner outreach, campus scheduling, intercity logistics, and on-site coordination through one lead team.

## Service Coverage

- **Institution partnerships**: support for outreach to universities and specialist institutions.
- **Exchange planning**: curriculum-aligned schedules, local facilitators, and campus coordination.
- **Cultural and language modules**: immersive activities that reinforce academic objectives.
- **Regional movement**: intercity transport planning and day-by-day timing control.
- **Risk and escalation support**: single point of contact for operational issues.

## Result

Your academic team focuses on learning outcomes while KaiLinks manages timing, logistics, and issue handling on-site.
