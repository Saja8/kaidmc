KaiLinks designs immersions for institutions that need to understand how universities, startups, corporates, and innovation communities connect in the target destination.

## What we handle

- **Ecosystem map**: selection of hubs, communities, institutions, and companies relevant to the group topic.
- **Innovation agenda**: sessions with startups, ecosystem operators, labs, corporates, or applied centres.
- **Use-case focus**: visits and discussions around technology, entrepreneurship, transfer, product design, or international collaboration.
- **Group preparation**: context briefing, visit objectives, and materials to make each meeting productive.
- **Follow-up**: summary of contacts, insights, and possible next collaboration steps.

## Who it's for

- Universities, incubators, entrepreneurship centres, and innovation programs.
- Delegations that need to understand the ecosystem before proposing partnerships or projects.
- Groups looking for applied content, not generic sightseeing.
