When a team or delegation travels abroad for a competition, tournament, or training camp, KaiLinks coordinates the logistics so the group arrives prepared and supported.

## What we handle

- **Registration and accreditation**: group registration, federation documentation, passes, and venue access.
- **Travel and accommodation**: hotels near the venue, meals, schedule timing, and team requirements.
- **Specialized transfers**: equipment transport, sports gear logistics, and movement between competition sites.
- **Side activities**: city familiarization, recovery activities, sponsor meetings, or scouting appointments.
- **On-site support**: local companion, liaison with event organizers, and issue resolution.

## Who it's for

- Amateur, school, university, and semi-professional teams traveling to international tournaments.
- Sports delegations with athletes, technical staff, and family groups.
- Coaches and agents coordinating participation for multiple athletes.
