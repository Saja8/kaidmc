Pavilion Mexico is a KaiLinks initiative that helps Mexican companies expand internationally through strategic expo participation, B2B networking, and market entry support — focused on Japan, China, and Germany.

## Services

- **Expo participation**: shared booth, logistics, multilingual staff, and full management at international trade shows such as Foodex Tokyo, Anuga Cologne, and Beauty World Japan.
- **Market entry**: market research, regulatory compliance, product localization, and distribution channels.
- **Strategic networking**: B2B meetings, connections with importers and distributors, and commercial partnership development.
- **Business tours**: immersive trips to target markets for direct exposure, cultural insights, and business engagement.
- **Cultural and legal guidance**: import/export compliance, business etiquette, translation, and contract negotiation.

## Industries

Food and beverage, technology, beauty and cosmetics, industrial, energy, medical devices, and more — with a proven track record in Asian markets, especially Japan.
