KaiLinks turns an exploratory visit into an organised market test: diagnosis, strategic contacts, B2B agenda, and clear deliverables before, during, and after the trip.

## What we handle

- **Entry diagnosis**: local ICP, adapted value proposition, priority channels, visible barriers, and initial regulatory questions.
- **Opportunity mapping**: importers, distributors, strategic partners, sector channels, incubators, and relevant commercial actors.
- **B2B agenda**: prioritized meetings with prospects, briefing for each session, interpretation, and on-site hosting.
- **Localized materials**: pitch, one-pager, and messaging adapted by contact type and target market.
- **Soft landing**: operational support during the stay — workspaces, ecosystem visits, and first-tier local contacts.
- **Commercial follow-up**: conversation summary, interest level, agreements reached, and recommended actions for the next 30–90 days.

## Who it's for

- Startups validating demand before setting up an office or hiring local agents.
- Exporters that need to understand channels, requirements, and potential partners in the target market.
- Commercial teams that need a structured market-entry agenda with clear follow-up.
