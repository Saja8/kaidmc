If you're an athlete, coach, or team representative traveling abroad for a competition, tournament, or training camp, KaiLinks handles all the logistics so you can focus on competing.

## What we handle

- **Registration and accreditation**: tournament sign-up, federation documentation, and venue access.
- **Travel and accommodation**: flights with sports equipment allowance, hotels near the venue, athlete-friendly meals and schedules.
- **Specialized transfers**: equipment transport, sports gear logistics, and movement between competition sites.
- **Side activities**: city familiarization, recovery activities, sponsor meetings, or scouting appointments.
- **On-site support**: local companion, liaison with event organizers, and issue resolution.

## Who it's for

- Individual athletes competing in marathons, triathlons, martial arts, golf, or tennis in other countries.
- Amateur and semi-professional teams traveling to international tournaments.
- Coaches and agents coordinating participation for multiple athletes.
