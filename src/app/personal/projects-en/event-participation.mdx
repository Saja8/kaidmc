If you work independently and want to attend an expo, conference, or professional event in another country, KaiLinks handles the logistics so you can focus on networking and making the most of the event.

## What we handle

- **Registration and access**: event sign-up, passes, accreditation, and B2B meeting scheduling.
- **Travel and accommodation**: flights, hotel near the venue, transfers, and optimized timing.
- **On-ground agenda**: itinerary combining the event with side meetings, market tours, or visits to potential clients.
- **Local support**: on-site companion or emergency contact in the event city.
- **Post-trip follow-up**: summary of contacts, leads, and suggested next steps.

## Who it's for

- Freelancers looking to exhibit or walk trade shows like Foodex, CES, Web Summit, or Anuga.
- Independent consultants attending sector-specific conferences abroad.
- Content creators, designers, and entrepreneurs seeking inspiration and international reach.
