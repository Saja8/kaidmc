Most event failures in new markets are operational, not strategic. The concept is usually strong. Execution gaps are the issue.

## Common Risk Zones

- **Vendor mismatch**: capability does not match proposal language.
- **Timing compression**: unrealistic transitions between agenda blocks.
- **Stakeholder drift**: changes approved without full impact review.
- **Communication lag**: delayed escalation on critical issues.

## Mitigation Actions

- Validate local suppliers against real delivery history.
- Build decision deadlines for each major dependency.
- Define escalation ownership before final confirmations.
- Run a final operational rehearsal with all active leads.
