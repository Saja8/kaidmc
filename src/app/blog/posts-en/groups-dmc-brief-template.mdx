Group programs are most efficient when the request is structured from day one.

## Brief Inputs to Include

- Group profile and support expectations.
- Fixed dates and flexible dates.
- Priority destinations and non-negotiable activities.
- Group size, pace, and operational constraints.
- Communication preference during execution.

## Recommended Workflow

1. Share a concise brief.
2. Review route and feasibility with your coordinator.
3. Lock sequence before booking activities.
4. Keep one communication channel live for changes.

This method prevents scope drift and reduces last-minute friction.
